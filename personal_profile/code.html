The Value of the Analog

In a world of infinite undo buttons and cloud backups, there is something profound about a physical photograph or a hand-brewed cup of coffee. You can't Command+Z a shutter click. You can't automate the taste of a roast.

These analog rituals ground me. They remind me that friction is often where the most beauty is found, and that taking the slow path usually leads to a more meaningful destination.
